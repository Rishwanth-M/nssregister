About Us

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a central initiative by the Government of India, under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It invites students from 11th & 12th grades, as well as those in technical institutions, colleges, and universities, to participate in government-led community service programs. Launched in 1969, NSS aims to shape the character and personality of students through voluntary community service. Operating across 657 universities, 51 councils/directorates, and involving over 20,000 institutions, NSS follows the motto of 'Education through Service.' Since its inception, more than 7.4 crore students have benefited from NSS.
